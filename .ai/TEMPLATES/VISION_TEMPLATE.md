# Product Vision — [Project Name]

> **Mode:** REFERENCE — Template for human use. Not an AI instruction.
>
> **Instructions:** Fill out each section below. Be concise but specific. This document is the seed from which your entire project grows. Aim for 1-2 pages total.

---

## Elevator Pitch

*One to two sentences that describe what your product is and why someone would want it.*

> **Example:** "A mobile app that connects dog owners with nearby dog-friendly restaurants, parks, and events, with real-time availability and reviews."

## Problem Statement

*What specific problem does this product solve? Who experiences this problem and how does it affect them?*

> **Example:** "Dog owners struggle to find places that welcome their pets. Existing apps show static lists that are often outdated, and users waste time calling ahead to confirm."

## Target Audience

*Who will use this product? Be as specific as possible: demographics, behaviors, needs.*

> **Example:** "Urban dog owners aged 25-45 who dine out regularly, use smartphone apps for discovery, and are active on social media."

## Core Value Proposition

*What makes this product different from alternatives? Why would someone choose it?*

> **Example:** "Real-time verified availability, user-submitted photos and reviews from actual dog owners, and a 'dog-friendly score' for each venue."

## Success Criteria (KPIs)

*How will you know if this product is successful? Define 3-5 measurable metrics.*

> **Example:**
> - 10,000 active users in the first 3 months
> - 80% of listed venues have verified dog-friendly status
> - Average user session > 5 minutes
> - 4.5+ star app store rating

## Future Vision (1-3 Years)

*Where do you see this product in 1-3 years? What features or capabilities will it have that it doesn't have now?*

> **Example:** "AI-powered recommendations based on dog breed and temperament, integration with pet service booking (grooming, vet visits), and a premium subscription tier for venues to feature their listing."

## Key Constraints

*What are your constraints? Budget, timeline, technology, team size, regulatory, etc.*

> **Example:**
> - Solo developer, 20 hours per week
> - Launch-ready MVP in 3 months
> - Must work on iOS and Android
> - No cloud budget — must use free tiers initially
> - Must comply with GDPR and CCPA from day one
